Connecting creative minds
Insprite is a community marketplace enabling people to discover, meet and learn from nearby makers, crafters and DIY enthusiasts first-hand.
Our Story
Ever start an artsy project and stop because it's not turning out the way you planned? It’s happened to us all too often.

One Pinterest fail after another, we realized maker skills would come easier if someone—a creative mentor—could help nudge us in the right direction when we needed it most. (Like the one time Christine and Sophie couldn't figure out why their silkscreening ran afoul even though they replicated each and every step from an online tutorial.) We wanted a home for connecting, meeting and learning from the cool, creative people around us—a place where we could find help getting started on a crafty project or obtaining guidance for those pesky, hair-pulling moments. We wanted a way to empower the makers, craftspeople and DIY enthusiasts creating beautiful work in their studios, apartments, homes, you name it, to impart their know-how, whether they were professionals or hobbyists, and get paid for it. From there, Insprite was born.

From hacking a mason jar lamp to mastering linocut printmaking and more, we bring you a DIT (do-it-together!) community marketplace connecting you to local maker artists who offer in-person, unique learning experiences you can't find elsewhere.
