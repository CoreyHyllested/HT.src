Our Story

Soulcrafting got its start as a community connecting consumers with master craftspeople for innovative, hands-on learning experiences. The community took shape, and people started building some really cool stuff. Over time, our customers began asking, can you help us feel connected to bigger projects for the home? A custom table? A new kitchen? Converting my garage into a studio to rent on Airbnb? The more we thought about it, the more we knew that we could.

We've witnessed firsthand how messy and stressful remodeling projects can be. The system is broken so we decided to redesign it from the ground up, using technology and a new set of values, to put customers first. With world-class customer service and smart collaboration tools, Soulcrafting is the easiest and safest way for people to manage their home improvement projects.

Common Questions

Where are you located?

Soulcrafting is based in Boulder, CO, and we're currently supporting projects throughout Boulder County. To learn when Soulcrafting is available in your area please connect with us on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.


How does Soulcrafting make money?

Our business model is based on shared success: we make a 10% service fee when homeowner's project is complete and they're satisfied that the job's been done right.



I'm a contractor. How do I get involved with Soulcrafting?

The most talented local craftspeople join our community by invitation only. If you'd like us to get to know you and your business create a Soulcrafting professional account.


Can I still sign up for Soulcrafting workshops?

We no longer offer regular workshops but many of our partners still do. You can find a complete list of craftspeople who are still offering classes.

How do I get started?

The best way to get started with Soulcrafting is to create and account and tell us a bit more about the project you have in mind. A member of our team will follow up with you directly to learn more about your project goals before connecting you with trusted contractors from our network.



How do I contact Soulcrafting?

Please feel free to contact us by email or by phone 779.456.6847. We'll do our best to respond to you right away.
