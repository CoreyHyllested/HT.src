Connecting Amazing People
Insprite is a online community helping people discover, meet, and learn from nearby artisans, makers, and experts first-hand.
Our Story
Have you ever had to stop a project because it's not working out? It’s happened to us. A lot.

One Pinterest fail after another, we realized some projects would be much easier if someone—a mentor—could help nudge us in the right direction. We wanted a home for connecting, meeting, and learning from the cool, passionate, experts around us. A place we could find guidance for those pesky, hair-pulling moments. We wanted to empower local experts to impart their know-how, whether they were professionals or hobbyists, and get paid for it. That was the birth of Insprite.

From making an amazing birthday cake to designing a patio to hacking a gardening project, we're showcasing local experts and mentors who offer in-person experiences you can't find elsewhere.
