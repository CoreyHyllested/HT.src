Upload High-Resolution Photo
We ask that all our teachers provide large, high-quality photos. A good-looking profile can make a big difference in attracting customers. Please make sure yours is at least 720 pixels wide, in landscape orientation (wider than it is tall), and has good lighting. If you're using a phone camera, make sure it's set to a high resolution. Ideally, the photo should be of you performing one of your skills, or of some your handiwork.
